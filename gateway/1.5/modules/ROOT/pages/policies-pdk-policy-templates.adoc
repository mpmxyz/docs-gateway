The Policy Development Kit (PDK) offers the following custom policy templates to get started configuring the Rust source code:

* xref:policies-pdk-template-caching-policy.adoc[]
+
Use the data caching policy template as an example of how to xref:policies-pdk-configure-features.adoc#share-data-between-workers-and-caching.adoc[configure policy caching].

* xref:policies-pdk-template-simple-oauth2-policy.adoc:
+
Use the simple OAuth 2.0 validation policy template as an example of how you create a policy that makes xref:policies-pdk-configure-features.adoc#perform-an-http-call[HTTP requests].
